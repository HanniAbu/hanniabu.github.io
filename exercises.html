Exercises

  • Intensity over quantity
  • Concentrate on keeping tension
  • Contract fast, release slow

Legs

Deadlifts

Bend knees when bar below knees, use hips when above

Squats

Legs/hips square, toes pointed slightly out, knees comfortably angled, keep shins and back parallel

Split Squats

One foot back on a bench, squat forward on other leg

Pulsing Lunges

Low weight, stay in low lunge position, pulse halway up and down

Cable Walks

Grab cable, stay in low squat position, walk back then forward

Monsters

Use resistance bands, lunge forward and over at an angle side to side

Back

Lat Pulldowns 1

Palms away, hands just past shoulders, bring elbows down/in

Lat Pulldowns 2

Palms in, hands @ shoulder width, bring elbows down/in, lengthen arm/elbow when bringing up

Trap Cable Pulls

Pulley down, cable @ side, pull shoulder up then rotate back/down

Trap Y-Lifts

Lean forward, hold dumbell w/ thumbs up, bring arms up in 'Y' position, rotate thumbs in and shoulders back/together while coming up

Armpit Pulldowns 3

Palm away, hands out as wide as possible, bring elbows down/back/in

Rotator Cuff W-Raises

Lean forward w/ elbows/bumbells/thumbs in, keep elbows @ 90, raise elbows up and rotate from thumb in to thumb up/back (goal is to keep elbows @ 90 and rotate arm/hand out)

Romboid T's

Lie face-down on bench, arms down with rounded bacck, reverse flys pinching shoulder blades back

Lower Back Extensions

Supermans or preacher goodmornings, linearly contracting lower back

Chest

Bench

Hold bar w/ grip at index or middle finger

Cable Crossovers

Start w/ elbow back and push across chest while rotating chest slightly. After set, hold in tension until burnout then do another set assisting to tension and slowly releasing

Dumbell Presses

Keep dumbells close to arm bit and bring together when raising

Weighted Dips

Lean forward slightly, drop slowly

One Arm Dumbell Press

Allows to concentrate more on muscle and bring dumbell in further

Shuffle Pushup

Do a lateral plank walk over, get down in pushup position, explode up while bringing up hips, tap your toes in w/o hand on floor, fall back into plank position and repeate

Pushup Burnout

Burnout with few sets of incline pushup, regular pushup, then decline pushup

Biceps

Dumbell Curls

Grip with thumb at edge of barbell, rotate from thumb up to thumb out

Dumbell Pause Curls

Grip with thumb at edge of barbell, rotate from thumb up to thumb out, start at midway, drop weight, lift weight, stop at center, alternate arms

Preacher Curls

High weight, low reps

Core

Thread the Needle

Sideways plank position, bring top hand from up in the air to crossing over and under, rotating body to reach back

Toe Tap

Sideways plank position, rotate body to bring foot back and tap the ground, then rotate body forward and tap in front

Hip Drop

Sideways plank position, rotate hips from angled up to dropping hips and angled down

Pull Up

Bench

Deadlifts

Barbell Curls

Contract fast, fight for slow drop

Overhead Dumbell Press

Start w/ elbows @ 90 and arms out, bring dumbells toward each other as raising elbows
