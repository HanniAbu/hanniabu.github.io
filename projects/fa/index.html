OUR STORY




Grace Anne Vigilia

Age 23

Hanni Abukhater

Age 24

We Are Adventurers

Ever since we met, a mutual love for animals and adventure have brought us closer together. Our time is spent showering animals with unconditional love and satisfying our hunger for adventure. We never were the type to be satisfied by the same mundane experiences over and over again. There is a burning desire within us to find that extra excitement, that change of scenery, and that extra dose of adventure.

And our furry friends are no different. An appreciation for adventure is not something only humans can possess, animals have it too! This is why we started Furry Adventures.
