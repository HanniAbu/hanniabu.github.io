ADVENTURE SITES



Brookdale Park, Bloomfield, NJ

Brookdale offers 121 acres of lush fields and trees with paved walking trails thoughout. There is also a dog park that provides a great social environment with breeds of all sizes for your critter to make friends with! It hosts a large enclosed area that allows your dog to roam free in the grass or sand and explore the obstacle courses provided.

Garret Mountain Reservation, Woodland Park, NJ

At a massive 568 acres, Garret Mountain s a great medium between a tradition park and a forest. With a combination of paved, dirt, and gravel paths there's adventures around every turn. The trails weave through wooded and open areas, by grass fields, and around a lake.

Visit Site

Eagle Rock Reservation, West Orange, NJ

Eagle Rock Reservation is a great environment with traditional paved trails throughout the park that wrap around and lead up to the 9/11 memorial at a hgih point overlooking the NYC skyline. There are also many dirt trails in between offering countless route possibilities.

Mills Reservation, Cedar Grove, NJ

This hidden oasis is only 157 acres, but hosts towering trees that completely submerge you in nature. Chipmunks and other critters are a common sight here and will be sure to get your little one even more excited.

Hilltop Reservation, Essex County, NJ

A common birding destination, Hilltop Reservation is a nice strip of woods between Verona and North Caldwell. This peaceful, flat terain contains many trails, some of which pass over streams, creating a very calming getaway.

Grace Anne Vigilia

We Are Adventurers

Ever since we met, a mutual love for animals and adventure have brought us closer together. Our time is spent showering animals with unconditional love and satisfying our hunger for adventure. We never were the type to be satisfied by the same mundane experiences over and over again. There is a burning desire within us to find that extra excitement, that change of scenery, and that extra dose of adventure.

And our furry friends are no different. An appreciation for adventure is not something only humans can possess, animals have it too! This is why we started Furry Adventures.
